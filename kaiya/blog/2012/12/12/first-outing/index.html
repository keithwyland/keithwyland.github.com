First Outing

Dec 12th, 2012

Before Carissa took on her current role of full-[double-, over-]time mom, she had a part time job as an administrative assistant at a local college. The same college both of us earned our degrees from. We brought Kaiya on her first outing (besides doctor visits) to the department Carissa worked in and showed her off a bit. All the while worrying about sick people sneezing on her, touching her, breathing on her, or being within 100 yards of her. Kaiya ended up not getting sick.
