About

I'm Keith, and this here's my blog. I like to learn, share knowledge about, and do front-end web development.

"I wish everyone in the world would blog." -Chris Coyier

Inspired by that statement and the rest of Chris's thought, I've decided I really need to get serious about the sharing of knowledge part by blogging.

So, also inspired by Dave Rupert, I went with a Jekyll site, built with HTML5 Boilerplate, and hosted on Github. No Wordpress plugins. No clunky FTP uploads. Nothing between me and sharing my experiences with anyone who may find them useful.
